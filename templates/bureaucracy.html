NagrikSahayak

Step-by-step guides for common bureaucratic processes.

How to File an FIR

First Information Report for cognizable offenses.
  1. 1

    Visit the Police Station

    Go to the police station having jurisdiction over the area.

  2. 2

    Written Complaint

    Submit a written complaint describing the incident in detail.

  3. 3

    Verification & Registration

    The officer will verify the details and register the FIR.

  4. ✓

    Get a Free Copy

    You are entitled to a free copy immediately.

How to File an RTI

Right to Information Act, 2005.
  1. 1

    Identify the Department

    Determine which Public Authority (Govt Dept) holds the information you need.

  2. 2

    Draft Application

    Write a simple application on plain paper or use rtionline.gov.in. Address it to the "Public Information Officer" (PIO).

  3. 3

    Pay the Fee

    Pay ₹10 via IPO, Demand Draft, or Online Payment (for Central Govt). BPL citizens are exempt.

  4. ✓

    Wait 30 Days

    The PIO must reply within 30 days. If not, you can file a 'First Appeal'.

Consumer Court Complaint

For defective goods or deficient services.
  1. 1

    Send Legal Notice

    Send a formal notice to the company/seller asking for redressal. Give them 15 days.

  2. 2

    File Complaint (E-Daakhil)

    If no response, file online at edaakhil.nic.in or visit the District Consumer Commission.

  3. ✓

    Attend Hearings

    You can argue your own case. No lawyer is mandatory.

Report Cyber Crime

Online fraud, harassment, or hacking.
  1. 1

    Gather Evidence

    Take screenshots of chats, emails, or transaction IDs immediately.

  2. 2

    Visit CyberCell

    Go to cybercrime.gov.in and click "File a Complaint".

  3. ✓

    Call 1930

    For financial fraud, call 1930 immediately to freeze the transaction.

Lost Property Report (NCR)

For lost Phones, SIMs, or Documents (Non-Cognizable Report).

  1. 1

    State Police Portal/App

    Visit your state police's app (e.g., UPCOP, Delhi Police Lost Report).

  2. 2

    Enter Details

    Fill in IMEI number (for phones), Aadhaar details, and place of loss.

  3. ✓

    Instant Certificate

    You will get a digitally signed NCR instantly. Use this to get a duplicate SIM or ID.

Marriage Registration

Official government record of marriage.
  1. 1

    Application

    Apply online via your State Govt's e-District portal or visit the SDM office.

  2. 2

    Visit Registrar

    Both partners and 2-3 witnesses must visit the office on the appointment date.

  3. ✓

    Issue of Certificate

    Certificate is usually issued on the same day or within a week.

Driving License

From Learner's to Permanent DL.
  1. 1

    Learner's License (LL)

    Apply at Sarathi Parivahan. Take an online test from home (Aadhaar based).

  2. 2

    Wait 30 Days

    You must hold the LL for at least 30 days before applying for a permanent DL.

  3. ✓

    Physical Test

    Book a slot, visit the RTO with your vehicle, pass the driving test, and the DL will be posted to you.

Passport Application

Fresh Application or Re-issue.
  1. 1

    Passport Seva

    Register on Passport Seva. Fill details meticulously.

  2. 2

    Appointment

    Pay the fee (₹1500) online and book an appointment at the nearest PSK.

  3. ✓

    Verification

    Visit PSK for biometrics. Police verification will follow at your address.

Startup India Registration

Get DPIIT recognition and tax benefits.
  1. 1

    Incorporate

    Register your business as a LLP or Pvt Ltd company.

  2. 2

    Startup India Portal

    Register on the portal, fill the form, and upload documents (PAN, Incorporation Cert).

  3. ✓

    Recognition

    Get your DPIIT certificate online. Enjoy 3-year tax holiday and self-compliance.

Legal Heir Certificate

To claim assets of a deceased family member.
  1. 1

    Application

    Apply to the Tehsildar or Revenue Officer. Attach Death Certificate and ID proofs.

  2. 2

    Affidavit

    Submit a self-undertaking affidavit detailing all surviving heirs.

  3. ✓

    Inquiry & Issue

    Revenue Inspector will visit for inquiry. Certificate is issued within 30 days.
